Jammu & Kashmir

4 Days/3 Nights

One of the coldest cities in India where you can look at many mountains covered in snow.

You will be picked up at Srinagar International Airport and transferred to the hotel in Srinagar where you will have your breakfast and later go on a city tour. You will also be taken onto some mountains and you will be joining a trekking group and reach the tip of the mountain and also camp there.

  • Airline charges including the breakfast on the plane.
  • Driver cum tour guide who will take to the places around.

  • Everyday morning breakfast buffet in the hotel.

Day-1

You will be picked up by our driver/tour guide who will take you to some waterfalls and some other amazing places on the way to the hotel. You can fresh up and have some amazing food and rest there for the day.

Day-2

How can you miss going on a snow fight when you are in Jammu & Kashmir? So you will be taken to a snow covered peak where you can make some snow angels or a snow man!

Day-3

You will get on a boat ride which will take you through a market where you can buy things to take back to home. Later, you will have all your meals on the house boat and stay in it for the night.

Day-4

You will be taken to Pangong Lake where you can see the clear water and have some fun there for the rest of the day. Later your will be dropped at the airport.
