Bangalore

4 Days/3 Nights

One of the best south indian city where you can experience many cultures.

You will be picked up at Kempegowda International Airport and transferred to the hotel in Bangalore where you will have your breakfast and later go on a city tour. You will also be taken to Nandi hills where you can have fun camping for a day.

Day-1

You will be picked up at the airport and transferred to the hotel where you can have a meal and fresh up. Later you will be taken on a city tour.

Day-2

You will be taken to Nandi hills early in the morning. You will be provided with 4 meals (breakfast, lunch, snacks and dinner) there. You can enjoy the fun activities like hiking, river rafting,etc. along with other people there. You can enjoy your night stay there in the camp in midst of a lovely place surrounded by greenery.

Day-3

You will be taken to all the monuments as well as temples on this day. Don't forget to get your camera for some beautiful shots!

Day-4

You will be having your breakfast at the hotel and later taken to some street where you can buy some souvenir to remember your visit.
