Delhi

3 Days/2 Nights

A visit to the capital city of India which enchants you to visit it again and again.

Day-1

The driver/tour guide will pick you up at the airport and take you to the hotel where you can have breakfast and fresh up as well. You will be taken on a city tour later.

Day-2

You will visit the most famous places in New Delhi, India gate, Red fort,Lotus Temple and Akshardam. You can enjoy your dinner in the hotel Parikrama.

Day-3

You will be taken to Palika Bazaar so that you can buy some souvenirs from your trip. And then you will be taken to taste some street food and dropped off at the airport at night.
