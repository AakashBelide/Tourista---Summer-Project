Kerala

5 Days/4 Nights

You can feel the cool breeze blowing through your face during this trip.

You will be picked up at Kochi Airport and transferred to the hotel in Munnar and on the way to Munnar you can have your breakfast and will be visiting few tourist spots as well. Don't forget to get your camera for some amazing clicks!

Places In Kerala

These are few of the places you will visit in Kerala during your 5 days stay!

Munnar

Thekkaday

Allepey Backwaters

Kochi

Day-1

You will be picked up at the airport and on the way to Munnar you can have breakfast. ALso you will look at some waterfalls and some beautiful mountains. You will be dropped at the hotel where you can stay for the rest of the day.

Day-2

You will be taken to the famous tea plantations where you can pose with the large tea plantations along with your family. Later, you will also be taken to some lakes and valleys. Enjoy your Kathakali show in the evening.

Day-3

Early int he morning after your breakfast, you will be taken to Thekkaday where you can go on safari and enjoy the elephant ride. After that, you can enjoy the Kalaripayattu show there in the evening.

Day-4

Next day morning after your breakfast, you will be taken to Allepey where you will be dropped off for a house boat ride and stay in it for the rest of the day and have a tight sleep there.

Day-5

In the morning, you will be transferred to Kochi where you will visit famous Chinese fishing nets and some churches. Later you can enjoy your visit to Lulu mall and then taken back to airport at the night.
