Shimla

3 Days/2 Nights

The weather in Shimla is always in favour of tourists and you can look at some amazing gardens and greenery.

You will be picked up at Jubbarhatti Airport and transferred to the hotel in Shimla where you will have your breakfast and later go on a city tour. You will be taken to some water falls as well as some gardens where you can have some cherishable memories.
