Day-1

The tour guide/driver will pick you up at the airport and take you to your hotel room where you can have your brunch.After freshing up, you will be taken on for a city tour in Berlin, Germany's capital city.

Day-2

Germany is known as the city of automobile. So, you will be taken to an automobile manufacturing plant where you can look yourself how an automobile especially how a car and it's parts are made and assembled step by step with the help of machines.

Day-3

You will be taken to the places where Adolf Hitler has been and visit his musuem to unveil and discover some amazing facts and unknown things about him.

Day-4

You will be taken to some Cathedrals as well as some old streets where you can find some kind locals selling their artwork. Do buy something to take it with you back to home.

Day-5

You will be taken to all the amazing food joints nearby where you can taste some really yummy food.In the evening, you will be dropped off at the airport.
