Places In Singapore

These are few of the places you will visit in Singapore during your 5 days stay!

Universal Studios

Merlion Statue

Gardens by the Bay

Sentosa Islands

Day-1

You will be picked up at the airport by our tour guide/driver who will take you to your hotel where you have have your breakfast and then ready up to go on a city tour on the tour bus along with other tourists.

Day-2

You will be taken to Sentosa Islands where you can spend the whole day enjoying all the adventurous rides and some tasty food. Later you will be provided a room in the Sentosa Islands resort where you can stay back for the rest of the night.

Day-3

You will be picked up at Sentosa Islands in the morning and taken to Universal Studios where you will be spending your time there till the evening discovering some amazing movie sets and stages as well as know about graphics and all the stuff related to the movies.

Day-4

Don't forget to meet the king of the jungle or the Merlion statue. Do pose with it and click a picture to remember about your amazing trip to Singapore!Later you will be spending your evening at the Gardens by the Bay where you will even have your dinner.

Day-5

You will be taken to the local streets where you can taste some amazing seafood and other fast foods. After eating, you will be provided with sometime to buy things you want to take back to home.In the evening, you will be dropped off at the airport.
