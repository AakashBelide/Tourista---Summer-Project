Day-1

You will be picked up at the airport and transferred to the hotel where you can fresh up and later hop onto the city tour bus to discover the surroundings.

Day-2

You will be taken to some amazing skyscrappers like Bhurj Khalifa, Burj Al Arab, etc. where you can enjoy an impeccable view. Don't forget to look down the transparent floor and don't be freaked out.

Day-3

You will spend your time on the beachside and later in the evening taken to a desert safari.You have to hop on a jeep and move in the woods for a jungle safari as well. Do click a picture with an eagle on your hand.

Day-4

You will be dropped off at the harbour where you can find your private ferry.You can meet the captain and a personal cook on the deck. You can ask them for anything you need and they try their best to provide you with the best possible experience.

Day-5

You will be taken to some botanical gardens and some sacred old musuems.Also taste some yummy street food before you get back to the hotel.

Day-6

You will be drived to some local merchants where you can buy clothes, electronics, footwear, watches, etc. at very cheap prices. Later in the night you will be dropped off at the airport.
