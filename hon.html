Day-1

You will be picked up at the airport by our tour guide/driver who will help you with the luggage and then transfer you to the hotel where you can have your breakfast and fresh up. Later you will be taken on a city tour with others ona tour bus.

Day-2

You will be taken to the foot of some mountains where you will be meeting other toruists and all of you will set for hiking to the peak of the mountain. Later at the top of the mountain, you can enjoy the cable cab ride to look at the amazing surroundings.

Day-3

In the morning after having your breakfast, you will be taken on river rafting through the woods where you can experience some beautiful places. In the evening you will be camping on the peak and then enjoy the food there along with a bonfire.

Day-4

On this day you will be visiting the famous Hong Kong Disneyland where your children can meet their childhood fantasy characters and take some great pictures with them. You will have to stay there for the night to experience night shows and also you can go around on your own.

Day-5

You will be taken to all the famous Cathedrals in the city as well as some musuems where you can have some peaceful time. In the evening, you will be taken to a 7-star resort cum hotel to enjoy your evening by the poolside along with some dj.

Day-6

You will be taken to the locals streets where you can taste some amazing food and buy wax candles and other handmade stuff from the locals who are very kind. At the night, you will be dropped off at the airport.
