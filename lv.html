Day-1

The tour guide/driver would come to pick you up at the airport and will then take you to the hotel where you can have proper lunch and then enjoy the poolside party till the evening at the casino.

Day-2

You will visit some amazing valleys where you can hike to the peak and camp there for the rest of the day. You will be provided with tasty food. AT night, you can enjoy star gazing.

Day-3

You will be taken onto a city tour from morning till around evening. You will be dropped at the hotel to fresh up and then will be taken to the casino where you can try your luck to make some bucks.

Day-4

You will be taken to some amazing studios where you can meet mediocre actors and also enjoy the street walks. During night, you will be taken to a concert being performed by some great artists.

Day-5

You can ask the tour guide to take to any particular place of your interest or go on a street shopping. In the evening, you will be dropped off at the airport.
