Venice

6 Days/5 Nights

Enjoy your ferry ride in the midst of calm and peaceful neighbourhood where you will fall in love with the city.

You will be picked up at the Venice Marco Polo Airport and transferred to the hotel where you can have your breakfast and take some rest. Later, go on a city walking tour and enjoy the dinner on a boat ride!

Places In Venice

These are few of the places you will visit in Venice during your 6 days stay!

St. Mark's Square

Rialto Bridge

Basilica di Santa Maria

Murano

Day-1

The tour guide/driver will pick you up at the aiport and drop you off for a ferry ride while your luggage is dropped off at the hotel.You will be taken to the hotel in the ferry where you can have your lunch and rest for the day.

Day-2

You will be picked up near your hotel to get onto to a ferry which will go around the whole city. Enjoy your ferry ride under the bridges. you can find some locals selling handmade stuff by the side which you can purchase.

Day-3

You will be taken to few musuems to discover and know about some interesting historical facts about the world as well as Venice.

Day-4

You will be visiting all the churches and the Murano where you can be all devotional and patriotic.

Day-5

You will be taken to the hills where you will have to hike to the top of the peak. Here you can show off your mountain climbing skills and also enjoy the barbecue grilled food at the top of the peak.

Day-6

You can ask your tour guide to take you to a particular place of your interest which is around and nearby. Later you will be dropped off at the airport.
